Ink cartridge challenges

Ink or toner issues can often happen if the cartridges aren’t installed correctly. You may also want to check for any clogged nozzles or dried-up toner that could be blocking up certain areas of your printed page.

Clogged Printhead

Symptoms of a clogged Printer printhead include streaky or faded prints, missing text or colors, and blurry documents. You may also see a message from your Printer or software that maintenance is needed, or that ink cartridges are full when no ink is printing.
